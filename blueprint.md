# Real Estate Agent Portfolio Website for Raj Kharel

## Overview

A professional and visually appealing portfolio website for Raj Kharel, a real estate agent at IKON Realty. The website will showcase his properties, highlight his expertise, and provide an easy way for potential clients to connect with him.

## Design and Features

*   **Theme:** Modern, professional, and trustworthy. The design will inspire confidence and highlight the agent's brand.
*   **Layout:** A single-page layout for easy navigation, with the following sections:
    *   **Hero Section:** A compelling introduction with a professional headshot of Raj Kharel, his name, title, and a strong call to action.
    *   **About Section:** A section detailing Raj's experience, expertise, and his commitment to clients.
    *   **Featured Listings Section:** A showcase of current or sold properties with high-quality images, brief descriptions, and key details.
    *   **Contact Section:** A clear and accessible contact form, along with his email and phone number.
*   **Styling:**
    *   **Color Palette:** A professional palette of blues, whites, and grays to create a sense of trust and sophistication. A gradient from gray-900 to gray-800 is used for the hero section, and a gradient from gray-50 to blue-50 is used for the listings section.
    *   **Typography:** Clean and readable sans-serif fonts for a modern look. `font-extrabold` is used for headings.
    *   **Imagery:** High-quality, professional images of properties and a professional headshot of the agent.
    *   **Animations:**
        *   A subtle "float" animation is added to the hero image to draw attention.
        *   Hover effects are used on buttons and links to provide visual feedback.
*   **Interactivity:**
    *   Smooth scrolling to different sections of the page.
    *   A functional contact form for lead generation.
    *   Interactive property listings that could potentially link to more detailed pages in the future.
